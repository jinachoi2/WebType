A South Korean dark comedy thriller film released in summer 2019 and directed by Bong Joon-ho. The movie follows the conflicts and shocking truths of two families: the economically struggling Kim family and the extremely wealthy Park family. The story begins when the son of the Kim family, Ki-woo seizes the opportunity to tutor the daughter of the Park family by forging his background...

Ki-woo
The eldest son in the Kim family. Becomes the English tutor for the daughter of the Park family, Da-Hye. He has a "plan".

Ki-jeong
The daughter of the Kim family. Becomes the art therapist for the son of the Park family, Da-song. Very skilled with Photoshop.

Ki-taek
The head of the Kim family. Becomes the chauffeur for acclaimed businessman and father of the Park family, Park Dong-ik. Very, very rich.

Chung-sook
The Mother of the Kim family. Becomes the housekeeper for the Park family. Used to be shotput athlete. Powerful and strong.
